Top Gadgets of 2016

We’ve seen a lot of pet robots over the years—but none were ever as cute or cheap as the $180 Anki Cozmo. With the help of a couple of Pixar animators, toymaker Anki built a robot with two motorized treads, big engaging eyes, and a ton of personality. The robot comes with built-in facial recognition, so it knows who it’s playing with. It also has a couple of simple games in which the little bot moves plastic cubes plastic around a table. When Cozmo loses, it throws the blocks around and sulks. Sore loser, huh?
